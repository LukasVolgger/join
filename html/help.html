How can you use Join App

  1. Fill in the task name on "Title".
  2. Click on calendar icon and select the due date.
  3. Select urgency by clicking on the respective fields.
  4. Fill in the description to descirpe the task.
  5. Click the "assigned to" you can choose other users who should work on that task.
  6. After filling up all the Inputs Fiels click on Create Task, you can find created tasks at "Backlog"
  7. In "Backlog" you can click on Task and move it to the "Board"
  8. In Board you can see the Task in "To Do" Section.
  9. You can move the Task from section to another by drag the Task with the Mouse and Drop it in the next Section.
